READYSETPREP VENMO SETUP

Before deploying payment.html:

1. Open payment.html.
2. Find:
   const VENMO_USERNAME='YOUR_VENMO_BUSINESS_USERNAME';
3. Replace YOUR_VENMO_BUSINESS_USERNAME with the real Venmo business username,
   without the @ symbol.

4. Find:
   const VENMO_PROFILE_URL='https://venmo.com/u/YOUR_VENMO_BUSINESS_USERNAME';
5. Replace the entire URL with the actual Venmo business-profile or payment URL.

Current workflow:
- Customer starts 3 days of local browser access.
- After the initial period, the customer sends $9.99 through Venmo.
- Customer enters the Venmo confirmation reference.
- The site stores payment status as “payment pending.”
- ReadySetPrep reviews and confirms the payment manually.

Important limitations:
- Venmo payments are not automatically verified.
- Monthly payments are not automatically charged.
- The static website does not securely enforce paid access.
- A backend or payment platform integration will be needed for reliable
  subscriptions, payment verification, cancellation, and access control.
