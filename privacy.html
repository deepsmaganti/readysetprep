ReadySetPrep Privacy Notice

This static preview does not send test answers, scores, assessment reports, or login passwords to a ReadySetPrep database.

The optional browser profile stores the entered display name and email only in this browser. The password field is used to demonstrate the login experience and is not stored. This is not secure production authentication.

Student names, answers, timing, progress, assessment history, question flags, and reading-passage highlights are also stored locally in the browser on the device being used. Clearing site data or using another browser or device removes or does not carry over this information.

The hosting provider may keep standard technical request logs, such as IP address, browser type, and access time, under its own terms and privacy policy.

For parents and teachers

Use a parent or guardian email for the optional browser profile. Use a child's initials, nickname, or teacher-assigned code inside practice tests instead of a full legal name. Do not enter phone numbers, home addresses, or other sensitive personal information.

The payment page uses a manual Venmo workflow. ReadySetPrep does not collect or store card details. The browser may store the trial dates, Venmo payment reference, and a local payment-status marker. The static site cannot verify Venmo payments automatically; payments must be reviewed manually.

For the private beta, ReadySetPrep may also store a local complimentary-access status after a valid access code is entered. In this static version, access-code validation happens in the browser and is not suitable as secure production authorization.

Complimentary access-code requests may also store a local request ID, requested access type, approval status, and approval timestamps. Contact messages and complimentary-access approval requests can be submitted directly through a server-side email function. The destination email address does not need to be displayed in the browser.

Before public launch

A production version should use a secure authentication provider, encrypted network connections, server-side payment verification, clear account and subscription controls, and a reviewed privacy policy appropriate for children and families.
