ReadySetPrep Privacy Notice

This version of the practice test does not create online student accounts and does not send test answers or scores to a central database.

The student name, answers, flags, timing, progress, generated practice assessments, assessment history, question flags, and reading-passage highlights are stored only in the web browser on the device being used. Clearing the browser's site data or using a different browser/device removes or does not carry over that saved progress.

The hosting provider may keep standard technical request logs, such as IP address, browser type, and access time, under its own terms and privacy policy.

For parents and teachers

Use initials, a nickname, or a teacher-assigned code instead of a child's full name. Do not enter email addresses, phone numbers, home addresses, or other sensitive personal information.
